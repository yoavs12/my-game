Game Modes

Mode 1

Play Against AI

This mode lets you play ping pong against a smart bot that adapts to your movements. Perfect for solo practice and improving your reflexes.

Mode 2

Play With a Friend

This option allows two players to play on the same computer using keyboard controls. A fun and competitive way to enjoy the game locally.

Mode 3

Online LAN Mode

Connect and play with another person on the same internet network. It's like playing real ping pong but over the local network!

About the Game

This game was built with love and JavaScript to bring the classic Ping Pong experience to your browser. It features multiple modes to match your style.
